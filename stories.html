🌈 The Rainbow Bridge Adventure

One sunny morning, five little animals—Lulu the lamb, Benny the bunny, Toto the turtle, Mia the mouse, and Rafi the raccoon—found a glowing rainbow stretching from their forest to the sky. “Let’s find out where it leads!” shouted Lulu. As they crossed the sparkling Rainbow Bridge, each color tested their friendship—Red made them argue, Blue made them feel sad, and Green made them sleepy. But with kindness, jokes, and holding paws, they made it through together. At the end, they found a cloud castle filled with singing flowers and cotton candy! “The real magic,” said Rafi, “was working as a team.” 🌟

🧠 Quiz Time!

1. What did the animals cross in the story?

2. What was the most important lesson?

🧭 The Map in Grandpa’s Attic

When Mira and Jay visited Grandpa, they climbed up to his dusty attic looking for old toys. Instead, they found an old scroll tied with string—a treasure map! With flashlights and backpacks, they followed the map through the garden gnome path, under the laundry vine, and behind the tool shed. There they found a hidden treehouse with a telescope, books, and snacks. “This was my secret clubhouse when I was your age,” Grandpa smiled. Now it’s yours to explore. Mira and Jay hugged him and named it "Adventure HQ." 🏕️

🧠 Quiz Time!

1. What did Mira and Jay find?

2. What was hidden behind the tool shed?
